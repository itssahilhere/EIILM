About CIEM

Discover our journey of excellence in education since 1995.

Leading the Way in Management Education

The Calcutta Institute of Engineering and Management (CIEM) was established with a vision to provide world-class education that blends theoretical knowledge with practical skills. Located in Kolkata, our institution has been at the forefront of nurturing future business leaders and technology innovators.

Our pedagogy focuses on integrated learning approaches that prepare students not just for their first job, but for a lifetime of career growth. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, CIEM offers an educational experience that truly transforms lives.
